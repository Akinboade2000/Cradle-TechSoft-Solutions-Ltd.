Who We Are

Technology Education Pioneers

Founded in 2024, Cradle TechSoft Solutions Limited is a cutting-edge technology education and consulting firm dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap in Nigeria and beyond. We combine world-class training with practical solutions to empower individuals and organizations in the digital age.

Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience in IT education, software development, and digital transformation, ensuring our clients receive the most current and relevant knowledge.
